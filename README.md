## Description

>Edwin, the goblin, is a very smart individual from Strongswordfell. He has a very anti-social behaviour. Edwin has no hobbies and is very proud about his smartness. One day Edwin decides that he has had enough of the lonely life he has in Strongswordfell. Furthermore he doesn't get respect from people. The villagers come to him to ask for help, they say their thank yous, but they never invite Edwin for anything. Edwin knows that he is a bitte cup cake and that he cannot control his feelings very easily. One day he has an amazing idea. He decides to go to the gym. He leaves his books behind, and with determination he ventures into the villages's Gym and starts to expore music, sport and healthy living. WIll he find the hapiness he desires? We will see. With this exercise we will help Edwin find his long gone social life and who knows, may we will help Edwin find love.
